E-commerce & Hi-Tech

Amruta Inc helps in multi channel marketing and ROI funnels, customer behaviour, data hub and exprience, online mobile and in-store shopping paths, purchase events, pricing and merchandising, store and warehouse locations and its operations, supplier/manufacturer interactions.

Solutions

Demand forecasting

Predict the most valuable data source, the company's internal data. The consumer demographic data analysed by AI tools helps the retail businesses define different contexts in which sales take place.

IT operations

Helps in increasing profitability and workforce productivity to reducing unplanned downtime and carbon emissions.

Pricing & Promotions

Prices are optimized considering forecasted demand, customer preferences, available product capacity, and product variations. The capacity is allocated and configured to maximize the profit and revenue by fulfilling the demand.

Marketing Attributions

XAI is used by marketers to help quantify the marketing impact of each individual marketing touch point across customer journeys. It also supports the two categories of scores, algorithmic and rule-based.

Use Cases

Diagnosing for channel conflicts

We rendered the customer journey from end to end, using online, call centre and offline data to identify the bottlenecks in order completions as well as offline poaching of online orders. Improved the e-commerce fulfilling process to prevent channel conflicts and overlaps.

Improving customer retention

We identified reasons for customer churn and implemented digital and mobile customer engagement treatments to improve customer retention. We also coordinated with call center operations to optimize omni-channel customner engagement while troubleshooting customer devices.

Improving IT infrastructure availability

We implemented ML based XAI platform to predict and prevent IT infrastructure failures. The reduction in the infrastructure failures improved e-commerce sales and customer satisfaction. The ML models predict the IT service/end point response times. The slower services are remedied.
