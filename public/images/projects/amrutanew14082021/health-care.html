Health Care

Amruta Inc is the provider of planning and operations and a payer of planning and operations.

Solutions

Providers- Hospitals

Emergency department throughout

It identifies if patient arrivals are unusually high compared to historical averages. It displays if the 'treat and release' or 'treat and admit' patients are the reason behind the congestion.

Length of stay and cost reduction

The DRG analysis is done to find the cost benefits and costs reductions. The reasons for length of stay are also identified.

Revenue cycle and value based payments

It helps by removing administrative waste due to inefficient revenue cycle practices, enhance decision-making, and improve patient engagement.

Pharmaceuticals reimbursement maximisation

Patient compliance is ensured and readmits are reduced. Revenue is increased and losses are minimized through charge master compliance and individual prescription profitability (total remittances minus drug cost) analysis.

Payers- Insurance companies

Claims fraud detection and adjudication

We implement data standardization, data transformation, reporting, prediction and optimization services in insurance claims processing. Also, Amruta PaaS (Platform as a Service) is implemented to detect fraudulent insurance claims.

Care & disease management and population health

The decision support systems are used in some shape or form though some of these techniques don't really fit in artificial intelligence definition or methodologies and many people define AI in completely different ways.

Use Cases

Real Time Monitoring

Enables to proactively identify congestion in the ER department. It displays the 'treat and release' or 'treat and admit' patient reasons.

Hospital star rating monitoring

CMS rates each hospital on a five-star rating system. The underlying measures and sub-measures across safety and quality outcomes, patient experience, through out and other categories are monitored. 

DRG Level Cost

The DRG analysis tools are implemented using ML algorithms to understand the cost benefits from various DRX codes, procedural changes, and physician's approaches.Cost drivers interms of floor cost and ancillary cost, including pharma and therapeutic cost are identified. Reason for increased lengths of stays are identified.
