Who are we and why should you learn from us?

At Amruta, we provide traditional and non-traditional data analytics applications that can solve problems in different sectors. Our new operationalized XAI is aimed to improve these applications to enhance revenue, profitability, reputation, safety and compliance of organizations. In order to help people build solid foundation in their AI & ML career, Amruta Academy has been established.

Beju Rao, the Founder and CEO of Amruta Academy, is an alumnus of IIT Kanpur. After functioning as a Research & Teaching Assistant at Texas A&M University for two years, he has worked as an Adjunct Professor at various universities. As an educator and entrepreneur, his aim is to mentor people of different capabilities with the help of Artificial Intelligence industry experts to make them job-ready through internships and real-life projects.

The Amruta Artificial Intelligence program enables you to unlock your potential as a professional by taking you through AI-based technologies such as Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Speech Recognition, Natural Language Processing and Reinforcement Learning.
